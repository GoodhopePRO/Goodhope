Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to visit Dubai every 6 months to keep my investor Visa valid?
As per the latest update from ICA you can stay unlimited time outside UAE.
What is the minimum value required for a 10-year Golden visa based on property? Is this property should be in Dubai?
As per Dubai Land Department, the mininmum value of the property should be atleast 2 million and the property should be in Dubai.
Is that mandatory to attest my relationship certificate to sponsor my dependents visa?
Yes. According to immigration, the certificate must be attested by the ministry of foreign affairs from which it was issued, as well as MOFA Dubai.
Do I need to be in the UAE to update my new passport details on my visa?
Yes the person should be inside UAE.
Is it necessary to have a local agent to initiate a new license in Dubai Mainland?
As per DED, it is not mandatory to have a local agent to initiate the license.
